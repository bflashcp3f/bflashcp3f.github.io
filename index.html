About Me

Hi! I am a CS Ph.D. student at Georgia Tech advised by professor Alan Ritter. Previously, I received my MS degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University and my BS degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University.

I have broad interests in natural language processing (NLP). My research has been focused on information extraction with minimal supervision (NAACL19), scientific protocol understanding (EACL21), and economical domain adaptation in NLP (EMNLP21).

Free feel to send me an email if you are interested in my research and would like to chat or collaborate!
