About Me

I am an incoming Postdoctoral Fellow in the Center for Language and Speech Processing at Johns Hopkins University, working with Prof. Mark Dredze. I got my Ph.D. at Georgia Tech with Prof. Alan Ritter.

Research Areas

  • Knowledge acquisition from multi-modal sources
  • Implementing AI systems under practical constraints
  • Learning models with synthetic data
  • LLM comprehension and application

Feel free to get in touch if you're interested in my research!
