About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech advised by professor Alan Ritter. Previously, I received my MS degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University and my BS degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University. My main research interest is to extract structured knowledge from large corpus using machine learning methods. Thanks for stopping by!
